MY SECOND PORTFOLIO



This portfolio contains the main assignments from my second semester as a graphic design student.

Before every main assignment I have been introduced to the relevant subjects, tutored and challenged via weekly assignments that I have posted on my reflection blog:

https://tonigiskemo.wordpress.com

My main outcome from the first semester was a basic understanding for design- principles and a sense for the esthetic. This has also been a curse because now I’m able to to see how bad both my early work and some of my current work is.

In the feedback for my first portfolio, it said that the design of the portfolio itself should have shown more of my design-personality. In this new portfolio I’m trying just that by going over my personal design-work to see what design-elements, fonts and colors I use on the average.

I still consider my self a newbie designer making the most out of basic design- principles, but now I have the ability to critic my self in a better way then before by learning from my past mistakes.

As a side-project I designed the packaging to a low-carb ice-cream for a highscholl-class who had it as a project.
